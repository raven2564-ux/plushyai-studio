Privacy Policy for PlushyAi Studio

Effective Date: June 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. PlushyAi Studio uses third-party authentication (Google Sign-In via Firebase) to allow you to create an account, save your generated creations, and manage your credit balance.

2. Information We Collect

We collect and securely store your email address and basic profile information solely for the purpose of account authentication and associating your generated images, stickers, videos, and 3D objects with your personal gallery.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly to provide the core functionality of the PlushyAi Studio application. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third-party advertisers or external marketing agencies.

4. Data Storage and Security

All files generated using PlushyAi Studio are securely stored in the cloud using Firebase Storage. They are only accessible by you when logged into your account, unless you explicitly choose to share or download them.

5. Consent

By using the PlushyAi Studio application, you consent to the processing of your data as described in this Privacy Policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Microsoft Store support channels.
